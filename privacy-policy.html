Privacy Policy for Volume Power Button

Last Updated: June 2026

Volume Power Button is developed by Fixo.9vd.

The App is designed to provide quick access to volume controls and power-related functions for users whose physical buttons are damaged or difficult to use.

The App does not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information.

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
