WiFixo Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

WiFixo is a mobile application designed to help users manage compatible Huawei routers from their Android devices.

Information Collection and Use

WiFixo does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to the developer.

  • Router IP address
  • Wi-Fi network name (SSID)
  • Router configuration settings
  • Connected device information
  • Temporary router session data required for authentication

This information is processed locally on the user's device and is used only to provide app functionality.

Data Storage

Router session information may be stored locally on the device to keep the user logged in to their router.

WiFixo does not store user data on external servers.

Data Sharing

WiFixo does not sell, share, rent, or disclose user information to third parties.

Permissions

WiFixo may require network-related permissions to:

  • Connect to the router
  • Read router information
  • Update router settings

Third-Party Services

The app may use Google Play services. Google may collect information according to its own privacy policies.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect information processed by the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

WiFixo is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
